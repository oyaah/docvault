# Benefits Overview

**Policy Owner:** Total Rewards Team  
**Last Updated:** 2025-09-15  
**Effective Date:** 2023-01-01

**Applies To:** All full-time team members

## 1. Benefits Philosophy

Acme Corp designs benefits to support the whole person — not just the employee. Our benefits package reflects three core beliefs:

1. **Health is foundational.** If you are worried about a medical bill or can't access care, you cannot do your best work.
2. **Financial security reduces anxiety.** Competitive compensation, retirement matching, and equity participation let team members focus forward.
3. **One size does not fit all.** A 25-year-old single engineer in Berlin and a 42-year-old parent of three in Texas have different needs. Our benefits flex to accommodate both.

## 2. Health Benefits (United States)

### 2.1 Medical Insurance

We offer three medical plans through Cigna. Acme Corp covers **100% of the employee premium** and **75% of dependent premiums** across all plans.

| Plan | Monthly Premium (Employee) | Monthly Premium (Employee + Family) | Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Max |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| PPO Premium | $0 | $312 | $500 individual / $1,000 family | $3,000 / $6,000 |
| PPO Standard | $0 | $198 | $1,500 individual / $3,000 family | $5,000 / $10,000 |
| HDHP + HSA | $0 | $145 | $2,800 individual / $5,600 family | $7,050 / $14,100 |

**HSA Contribution:** For team members enrolled in the HDHP, Acme Corp contributes $1,200/year (individual) or $2,400/year (family) to your Health Savings Account, deposited quarterly.

### 2.2 Dental Insurance

Provided through Delta Dental. Acme Corp covers 100% of the employee premium and 75% of dependent premiums.

- **Preventive care:** 100% covered (cleanings, exams, X-rays)
- **Basic procedures:** 80% covered after $50 deductible
- **Major procedures:** 50% covered after $50 deductible
- **Orthodontia:** 50% covered, $2,500 lifetime max (dependents under 19)
- **Annual maximum:** $2,000 per person

### 2.3 Vision Insurance

Provided through VSP. Acme Corp covers 100% of all premiums.

- **Eye exams:** $10 copay, once per 12 months
- **Lenses:** $25 copay, once per 12 months
- **Frames:** $150 allowance, once per 24 months
- **Contact lenses:** $150 allowance in lieu of frames

### 2.4 Life and AD&D Insurance

- **Basic life insurance:** 2x annual salary, up to $500,000 (company-paid)
- **Basic AD&D:** 2x annual salary, up to $500,000 (company-paid)
- **Supplemental life insurance:** Available for purchase up to 5x salary, max $1,000,000 (evidence of insurability required above 3x)
- **Spouse/dependent life:** Available for purchase ($25,000 / $10,000)

### 2.5 Disability Insurance

- **Short-term disability:** 60% of base salary, up to $2,500/week, 26-week maximum (company-paid)
- **Long-term disability:** 60% of base salary, up to $15,000/month, benefits begin after 90 days (company-paid)

## 3. Health Benefits (International)

### 3.1 Countries with Statutory Health Systems

In countries with public healthcare (UK, Germany, Canada, Australia, etc.), Acme Corp:

- Pays all required employer contributions to the national health system
- Provides supplemental private health insurance through Cigna Global
- Supplemental coverage includes private hospital rooms, specialist referrals without wait times, dental, and vision

### 3.2 Countries without Statutory Health Systems

For team members in countries without comprehensive public health coverage, Acme Corp provides:

- Full private health insurance through Cigna Global
- Coverage equivalent to the US PPO Premium plan
- Dependents covered at 75% of premium cost

### 3.3 Global Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

All team members worldwide have access to the EAP, which includes:

- 24/7 crisis support hotline (multilingual)
- 6 counseling sessions per year per issue
- Legal consultation (30-minute sessions)
- Financial planning consultation
- Work-life referral services (childcare, eldercare, moving assistance)

## 4. Mental Health and Wellness

### 4.1 Modern Health

Every team member and their dependents (age 6+) have access to Modern Health:

- **Therapy:** 6 sessions per person per year with licensed therapists
- **Coaching:** Unlimited sessions with certified coaches
- **Digital programs:** Self-guided courses on stress, sleep, relationships, and more
- **Circles:** Group sessions led by therapists on specific topics

### 4.2 Wellness Stipend

$100 USD per month (or local equivalent) for wellness-related expenses:

- Gym memberships and fitness classes
- Home fitness equipment (up to $300 in a single purchase)
- Meditation and mindfulness apps (Headspace, Calm, etc.)
- Nutrition counseling
- Ergonomic accessories not covered by the home office stipend
- Spa and massage services

Submit receipts through Expensify with the category "Wellness Stipend."

### 4.3 No-Meeting Fridays

Every Friday is meeting-free company-wide. This protects deep work time and gives everyone a buffer day before the weekend. Exceptions require director-level approval and should be rare.

## 5. Financial Benefits

### 5.1 Compensation Structure

Total compensation at Acme Corp consists of:

1. **Base salary:** Determined by role, level, and location factor
2. **Equity:** Stock options or RSUs depending on stage and role
3. **Variable compensation:** For roles with quota (Sales, Customer Success)
4. **Benefits and perks:** Everything described in this document

### 5.2 Retirement Plans

**United States — 401(k):**
- Acme Corp matches 100% of contributions up to 4% of salary, plus 50% of the next 2% (effective match: 5% at 6% contribution)
- Immediate vesting — no vesting schedule
- Both traditional and Roth options available
- Plan administrator: Fidelity

**United Kingdom — Pension:**
- Acme Corp contributes 5% of qualifying earnings
- Employee minimum contribution: 3% (statutory)
- Provider: Aviva

**Other Countries:**
- We contribute to statutory retirement/pension systems as required by law
- Where voluntary supplemental schemes exist, we contribute at competitive local market rates

### 5.3 Equity

All full-time team members receive equity as part of their compensation package.

- **Stock options:** 4-year vesting schedule with a 1-year cliff
- **Refresh grants:** Eligible after 2 years, reviewed annually during compensation cycles
- **Exercise window:** 10 years post-termination for options (extended from the standard 90 days)
- **409A valuation:** Updated quarterly; current FMV available on request from Finance

### 5.4 Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)

- Purchase company stock at a 15% discount to market price
- Enrollment windows: January and July
- Maximum contribution: 10% of base salary or $25,000/year, whichever is less
- 6-month purchase periods

### 5.5 Financial Planning

All team members have access to:

- **Origin Financial:** Unlimited 1:1 financial planning sessions
- **Tax preparation assistance:** Reimbursement of up to $500/year for tax preparation services
- **Equity education:** Quarterly workshops on understanding stock options, tax implications, and financial planning around equity events

### 6.4 Adoption and Surrogacy Reimbursement

Up to $10,000 reimbursement for adoption-related expenses and up to $10,000 for surrogacy-related expenses (in addition to Carrot Fertility benefit). Covers legal fees, court costs, agency fees, travel, and related expenses.

## 10. Eligibility and Enrollment

### 10.1 Eligibility

- **Full-time team members:** Eligible for all benefits on day 1
- **Part-time team members (20+ hours/week):** Eligible for health benefits and prorated stipends
- **Part-time team members (<20 hours/week):** Eligible for EAP and statutory benefits only
- **Contractors:** Not eligible for benefits (see contractor agreement for details)

### 10.2 Open Enrollment

Open enrollment for health benefits occurs annually in November, effective January 1. Outside of open enrollment, benefits changes are only permitted for qualifying life events:

- Marriage or domestic partnership
- Divorce or dissolution of domestic partnership
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Relocation to a new coverage area

Qualifying life event changes must be submitted within 30 days of the event.

### 10.3 Benefits Upon Separation

- **Health insurance (US):** COBRA continuation available for up to 18 months; Acme Corp subsidizes 3 months of COBRA premiums for involuntary separations
- **Equity:** See equity agreement for post-termination exercise details (10-year window for options)
- **Stipends:** Prorated to last day of employment
- **Retirement:** 401(k) balance is yours to keep (immediate vesting)
